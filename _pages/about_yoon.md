<strong>Sean Yoon</strong> (PI):
I am a physical hydrologist specializing in the study of hydrogeological systems, with a specific focus on flow and reactive transport phenomena occurring within porous and fractured media. My research integrates theoretical, computational, experimental, and field-based methodologies, all aimed at enhancing our foundational understanding of these phenomena across various scales, ranging from microscopic to field scales. I obtained my MSc in 2009 from Yonsei University and my Ph.D. in 2016 from MIT, both in Civil and Environmental Engineering. For the upcoming 2024 spring semester, I will be teaching Groundwater Geology (GLY 4822).
