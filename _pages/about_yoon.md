<strong>Seonkyoo (Sean) Yoon</strong> (PI):
I am a physical hydrologist specializing in the study of flow and reactive transport phenomena within porous and fractured media. My research approach combines theoretical, computational, experimental, and field-based methods to deepen our understanding of these phenomena across different scales, from the microscopic to the field level. I earned my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in 2007 and 2009 from Yonsei University, and my Ph.D. in 2016 from MIT, all in Civil and Environmental Engineering.
In the upcoming 2024 spring semester, I will be teaching Groundwater Geology (GLY 4822).<br>
